Grouping up

Calls

"Group up"

Numbers advantage is the key to Overwatch. Grouping up before contesting the objective gives you the best chance of winning the fight.

It doesn't mean the team needs to be in a ball. It means the entire team, or as much as possible (if you're short on time) is ready to fight at the objective - together.

It can be unavoidable, but if you otherwise die while "group up" is being called - You fucked up.
