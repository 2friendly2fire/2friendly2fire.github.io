Grouping up

Calls

"Group up": Gather as a team so that we contest the objective with maximum numbers.

Why group up?

Numbers advantage is the key to Overwatch. Grouping up before contesting the objective gives you the best chance of winning the fight.

It doesn't mean the team needs to be in a ball. It means the entire team, or as much of it as possible (if you're short on time) is ready to fight at the objective - together.

Take the call seriously

It can be unavoidable, but if you otherwise die after "group up" has being called - You fucked up.
