The Objective

You win a comp game by holding the objective.

Not kills, not medals, not stats.

You win by pushing the payload further than the enemy team, by being king of the hill, or by capturing the most ticks on a point.

The Contest-Regroup cycle

Numbers advantage combined with playing the objective produces the repeating flow of a comp match:

Group up for numbers

Contest the objective with numbers

...repeat

If your team WINS the objective after contesting, your goal is to increase your objective time by MAXIMIZING the time until the enemy team can contest the objective with numbers.

If your team LOSES the objective, your goal is to MINIMIZE the time until you can contest the objective with numbers.

This is the structure of an Overwatch comp match.
