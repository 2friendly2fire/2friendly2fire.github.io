The Objective

You win a comp game by holding the objective.

Not kills, not medals, not stats.

You win by pushing the payload further than the enemy team, by being king of the hill, or by capturing the most points.

The concept of Hero advantage combined with playing the objective produces the flow of a comp match:

Group up for numbers
Contest the objective with numbers

If your team WINS the objective, your goal is to increase your objective time by MAXIMIZING the time until the enemy team can again contest the objective with numbers.

If your team LOSES the objective, your goal is to MINIMIZE the time until you can again contest the objective with numbers.

This is the dynamic of an overwatch match.
