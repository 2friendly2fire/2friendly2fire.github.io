How to start playing Competitive in Overwatch

This site will teach the foundations of comp that should keep the pyrrhic defeats to a minimum.

Foundations

These are the concepts that produce the "Contest and Regroup" cycle of a competitive match.

  1. Hero advantage
  2. The objective
  3. A game of yards
  4. Ultimate economy
  5. Not tilting

Contest and Regroup: Tactics and calls

Each part of the Contest and Regroup cycle has common voice calls, each related to what the team should be doing in that part of the Contest and Regroup cycle.

  1. "Group up" Grouping-up
  2. "Point!" On the objective
  3. "Chase", "Stagger" Staggering
  4. "Don't get too aggressive" Not getting staggered
  5. "Don't use ults" Not wasting ults
  6. "Rein! Rein!", "Soldier is low", "Shield" Targets
  7. [No specific call] Not dying
