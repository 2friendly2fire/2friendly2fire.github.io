Hero advantage

Almost everything that happens in competitive results from the power of numbers advantage.

The team with more players alive in the fight has a significant advantage.

The advantage of the team with superior numbers snowballs as the numbers become more uneven. Gaming math has it that it's exponential.

Why do people hate "leavers" in competitive?

If a player drops in comp so that the game becomes a 5v6, the team with 5 will almost NEVER win. It would be a stunning defeat for the 6 player team were this to happen.

This power dynamic is also present throughout each fight in a normal game - whenever a numbers advantage exists for either team.

Comp is about using this basic feature of the game to win the objective.
