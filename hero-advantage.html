Hero advantage

Almost everything that happens in competitive is rooted in the concept of numbers advantage.

The team with more alive players - in the fight - has a significant advantage.

The advantage of the team with superior numbers snowballs as the numbers become more uneven.

To make the point, if a player drops in comp so that the game becomes a 5v6, the team with 5 will almost NEVER win. It would be a stunning defeat for the 6 player team, were this to happen.

This power dynamic is thus also present throughout the game.

Comp is about using this basic feature of the game to win the objective.
