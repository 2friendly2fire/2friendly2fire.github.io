Not getting staggered

Calls

"Reset": We've lost the fight, everyone retreat and regroup.

"[passive aggressive]Regroup...": You messed up.

"Winnable!": I don't think the fight for the objective is lost, don't worry about getting staggered, keep contesting.

If you DO NOT hold the objective, your primary goal is to regroup as quickly as possible to contest the objective again.

Getting killed (when it is avoidable) after "regroup" or "reset" is called is pretty much the biggest mistake in Overwatch. It's also one of the most common.

If your team is regrouping for the next fight, the only thing you should be doing is backing the fuck up to the team.

If you get killed during this phase, really the only thing you can say is "Damn." There isn't really anything else as useful as staying alive during this phase.

A more advanced tactic is to build ult during this phase, but ... just better not.

The "winnable" call means that it's someone's opinion that the contest phase is not over, and that the objective might still be won.

Until "regroup" is called, anyone alive should be fighting for the objective and anyone respawning should be running in to help as fast as possible.
